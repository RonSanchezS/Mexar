Our Services

Office Cleaning

We know that arriving at a clean and organized workplace predisposes us in a good mood to start any activity. That is why we offer our office cleaning service (bathroom, desk, floors, kitchen, and carpet vacuuming)

Construction Cleaning

Our cleaning service involves removing all the debris, dust, and leftover materials that are left behind after a construction project. This type of cleaning involves a deep clean of the entire site, both inside and outside the building.

Building Maintenance

Our building services take care of cleaning common areas, carpet vacuuming, regular trash removal, and repairing broken items.

Window Cleaning

We can perform commercial, residential, and post-construction window cleaning. We clean their interior and exterior.

Pressure Washing

We remove oil stains, paint, and dirt in general. Pressure washing will prevent premature aging of the floor.
Regular Pressure washing maintains the life of your floors.

Carpet Cleaning

We offer to remove stains, dirt, and allergens from carpets. Common methods include hot water extraction, dry cleaning, and vacuuming.

Floor Care

Our commercial and residential floor cleaning and stripping services allow us to remove old coatings and apply a brand-new coat of protective wax. We can clean and treat everything from concrete floors to warehouse flooring.

Maid Services

We offer a unique cleaning service where work surfaces and common and living spaces can be enjoyed by its occupants.

Snow Removal

Our snow removal service includes removing snow from sidewalks, parking lots and common areas. We know how hard that job can be, leave it to us!!

Sanitation

Our sanitation service eliminates viruses and other biological agents in the air and on any surfaces within the area that's being treated. Widely used in childcare centers, schools, and offices.
